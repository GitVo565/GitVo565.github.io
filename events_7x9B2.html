‚ÑπÔ∏è Wrestling 101: Event Format Guide

Multi-Team Meet (Scramble): No fixed brackets. Wrestlers are paired up based on weight/skill for 2-3 matches. Often used when team sizes vary.

Dual Meet: Head-to-head team battle (e.g., Point vs. Marshfield). One match per weight class. Team score is kept.

Invitational (Tournament): Traditional brackets. Wrestlers compete to advance (Quarters -> Semis -> Finals) to win a medal. Longest duration event.
