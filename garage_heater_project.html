Phase 2: Maintenance Swap (Jan 2026)
Replacement & Repair

Objective: Replace failing unit (lost "Low" stage heat) with identical hardware to avoid rewiring.

Installation Strategy

  • Direct Swap: The existing U-shaped ceiling bracket was NOT removed. The new body was lifted directly into the old mount.
  • Conduit Transfer: Old locknut was seized. Used "Tap and Twist" method (flathead + hammer) to loosen without stripping wires.
  • Wiring Confirmation:
    • L1/L2: Black & White/Red wires ‚Üí Top Terminal.
