Appendix E: Definitions & Algorithms
"Consistency > Intensity."

We do not chase "Hero Workouts." A "B+" workout done 50 weeks a year beats an "A+" workout done for 3 weeks before burnout.
The "Check the Box" Rule: If tired, reduce intensity to Zone 1, get the work done, keep the rhythm alive.

üèÉ The Running Menu

  • Zone 1 (Flush): "Guiltily Slow" / Medicinal. Mouth closed.
  • Zone 2 (Base): Conversational. The engine builder.
  • Threshold (T): "Comfortably Hard." 2-3 word sentences.
  • Strides (ST): 100m accelerations. 90% speed, relaxed form.
  • Hills (Short): 8-10s max effort sprints. Power output. Full recovery.
  • Flying 30s: Build 30m, Sprint Max 30m, Float out. Max Velocity.

üõë The "Wrestling Filter" (Adjustment Logic)

Scenario A: "The Grinder" (Hard Practice) CANCEL Run > 3mi. Switch to Flush/Off.
Scenario B: "The Drill" (Tech Practice) GREEN LIGHT for scheduled work.
Scenario C: "The Bump" (Minor Pain < 3/10) MODIFY (Bike).
Scenario D: "The Injury" (Pain > 3/10) FULL STOP. Immune Protocol.

‚öñÔ∏è Scheduling Constraints

  • SUNDAY IS SACRED (The Sunday Law): OFF. No lifting. No running. No "Active Recovery" flushes. Absolute CNS Reset.
  • 48-Hour Rule: No heavy leg lifting (Lift A/B) within 48 hours of Competition.
  • WINTER PROTOCOL: NO MORNING RUNS. All runs occur PM at 212 Fitness (post-practice) or on weekends. Sleep is the priority.

üåä Load Management (The Wave)

  • The Concept: We do not grind linearly. We utilize a "Stress Wave."
  • The Reload (Deload): Occurs every 3-4 weeks (e.g., Feb 2-8).
  • The Trigger: Usually scheduled after a multi-meet week ("The Gauntlet").
  • The Protocol: Reduce competition/sprint stress. Re-introduce structural lifting (Lift B). Allow supercompensation.
