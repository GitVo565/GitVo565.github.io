Tacie Voetberg Wrestling Recap: Edgar CWWC Meet (February 9, 2026)

We had a packed house at Edgar Middle School on Monday night for the CWWC Meet. Tacie wrestled three matches, showing continued progression and aggression on the mat.

Match 1: vs. Jenna Heise (Horace Mann) Tacie came out strong against Jenna Heise. She focused on staying aggressive while maintaining a solid game plan. She secured a pin in the first period at 1:32.

Match 2: vs. Natalie Morey (Horace Mann) In her second match, Tacie faced an 8th grader, Natalie Morey. This bout went the distance, lasting all three rounds. Tacie was the aggressor throughout, controlling the match and not giving up any points. She won by Major Decision, 8-0.

Match 3: vs. Livia Gardner (Marshfield) Tacie's final match of the night was against Livia Gardner, a very experienced club wrestler. Livia is technically skilled and has been wrestling for some time. Tacie made it through the first round but was pinned in the second at 1:23. While it was a loss, matches against experienced opponents like this provide valuable video for us to study.

Overall Thoughts It is important to remember that this is Tacie's first year of wrestling, while her primary sport remains running. Just as a new runner would struggle against Tacie, she will occasionally face wrestlers who have been on the mat for years. The goal isn't to win every match, but to close the gap with experienced opponents.

Most importantly, Tacie's sportsmanship and dedication were excellent. Between matches, she was mat-side cheering on teammates and listening to coaching. She is actively watching film to improve her technique and is clearly having fun with the process.
