Infrastructure & Engineering
The Power Backbone "Chasing the Demon Hum"
  • 7 Dedicated Circuits: Ensures zero light flicker during high-SPL bass drops.
  • Subwoofer Array: Each Klipsch sub (1600W) has its own 20-amp line.
  • "Clean Leg" Strategy: All audio gear (Denon/Outlaw/Subs) installed on the same electrical phase to eliminate ground loops.
  • The "Snack Circuit": Isolated rear-wall circuit for popcorn/appliances to prevent tripping the amps.
